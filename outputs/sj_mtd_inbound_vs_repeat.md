## Working hypotheses to investigate

1. **Routing change?** Both agents were Unknown-budget closers in 2025; both have closed zero such deals in April 2026. Check whether high-end inbound leads in April 2026 are being routed to other agents (Mandy Lubrano, Sarah Angry, and Katie Carson all picked up Unknown-budget bookings this April).
2. **Pipeline stalls?** Both agents may have high-end inbound deals stuck in proposal/offer that haven't contracted yet. Pull their open `proposal_stage_started_at IS NOT NULL` and `offer_stage_started_at IS NOT NULL` opps in the >$50K and Unknown buckets.
3. **Capacity shift to repeat?** Both agents grew repeat substantially, especially Sawyer (gross +$237K). Possible they intentionally focused on repeat business this April — worth confirming whether this was directed or organic.
4. **Sample size caveat:** these are 4-week windows on a small number of high-end bookings (3 Unknown for Sawyer, 2 Unknown for Jessica in 2025). One quarter of normal flow could look like a collapse. Check Q1 and rolling 90-day to see if this is a one-month blip or a trend.
