## What's driving the April miss

- **Top of funnel is healthy.** Inbound leads 1,311 vs 1,164 LY (+13%); inbound assigned 1,095 vs 976 LY (+12%). Demand isn't the problem.
- **Repeat volume is down materially.** 302 repeat opps vs 446 LY (-32%) and 302 vs 446 LM (-24%). Yet repeat commission is still up YoY ($829,285 vs $697,138, +19%) — fewer but larger repeat deals are landing.
- **The miss is concentrated in inbound revenue.** Inbound commission $551,825 vs $879,203 LY (-37%) on 88 bookings vs 83 LY (+6%) — more bookings, less revenue, so deal size has compressed.
- **Average $/booking dropped.** Apr-26 $8,221 vs Apr-25 $10,105 (-19%), Mar-26 $8,890 (-8%).
- **Biggest agent-level commission drops YoY:** Sawyer Panara ($110,950 vs $383,050, -71%), Jessica Brown ($65,925 vs $191,625, -66%), Mandy Lubrano ($304,750 vs $348,275, -12%).
- **Biggest agent-level commission gains YoY:** Sarah Angry ($227,500 vs $101,750, +124%), Katie Carson ($164,560 vs $48,688, +238%), Amanda Miller ($177,275 vs $139,500, +27%).
- **Inbound routing shifts (YoY assigned counts):** down to Jessica Brown (16 vs 110 LY), Bridget Barrett (32 vs 75 LY), Sawyer Panara (93 vs 116 LY); up to Amanda Miller (130 vs 73 LY), Jasmine Mansfield (117 vs 72 LY), Alexis Ulrey (38 vs 0 LY).
